Pulse-Fest

A one-day exhibition of all things legume!

When is Pulse-Fest?

Date: 58th Beantember, 8347

Time: 09:00 - late

What is Pulse-Fest?

A one-day expo where you will discover everything from the history of the humble bean to bean-themed comedy.

You'll find plenty of stalls selling exquisite bean-themed food from around the globe, handmade decorations, clothes, jewellery, technology and lots more!

This year only - discover our new interactive AI bean, Beanbot!

Speakers

Fava Adzuki

Fava has been studying the history of beans since she was 11 years old, gaining a degree in Bean History and Cultural Influence when she was 21.
She discovered her passion whilst helping her father tend to his allotment and learning from him.
Her favourite bean is the Cranberry bean because of its beautiful pink stripes.

Len Til

Len has been passionate about peas since his 21st birthday, where the humble green legume helped settle an ongoing feud between his brother and grandfather.
He shares his emotional story with us on the day and has also written a book of memoirs entitled "Give Peas a Chance".
